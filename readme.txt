Help support the Breast Cancer Awareness wareness by putting a ribbon on your WordPress site or network.


== Description ==


A small ribbon will be put on in the top right corner of your website with the Breast Cancer Awareness Ribbon and "Help Support the Fight Against Breast Cancer" will be put on in the top right corner of your website, linking to the Breast Cancer Awareness Facebook page (http://www.facebook.com/BreastCancerAwareness).
== Installation ==


1. Download the plugin zip archive.

2. Extract it in your `wp-content/plugins` folder or upload via admin panel.

3. Browse to your plugins section and active the plugin.
